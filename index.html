Our History

La Consolacion College Novaliches (LCCN), formerly La Consolacion College - Deparo, is one of 24 esteemed institutions administered by the Augustinian Sisters of Our Lady of Consolation (ASOLC). Established in February 1995, we proudly stand as the first Catholic educational institution in the area. Our name was updated to better represent our community and mission in the heart of Novaliches. As a member of the Inter-School Basketball and Volleyball League (ISBVL), we champion both academic and athletic excellence.

A Message of Hope and Progress

Warmest greetings of peace and love to the entire LCC Novaliches community.

As we navigate the challenges of the 21st century, we continue to draw strength from our core values of Truth, Unity, and Charity. Our mission is to provide a holistic formation for our youth, inspired by the virtues of Mary, Our Lady of Consolation.

We are committed to re-inventing the future of education while honoring our venerable traditions. We journey forward with our dedicated personnel and supportive families, fostering an environment where every student can thrive. Together, we strive to have "one heart and one mind on the way to God."

To our current and future LCCians, let us continue to "act justly, love tenderly, and walk humbly with our God" in all our endeavors. Mabuhay ang LCC Novaliches!
